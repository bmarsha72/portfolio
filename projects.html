Hate State Navigate

Hate State Navigateclose

Created the week following North Carolina's foray into bathroom law idiocy, this project's aim was to allow businesses to register their bathrooms as transgender accessible and offer a pin-dropping search function for users. I came up with the idea, having watched the story unfold on the news feed while riding the El in the morning. I also assisted with the Google map API, and the backend routes. My favorite part, aside from learning from my group, is the graphic on the front with the three bathroom doors. With more strange bathroom laws cropping up all over the country, this project may get revitalized in the near future, although it's not currently live.

Mu/Chart Generator

Mu/Chart Generatorclose

Inspired by Essential Charts, Mu/Chart Generator is an app that allows users to build and share music album flowcharts. This generator is an easy way to visually categorize your musical tastes. I loved working in this group, as I got to learn about React drag and drop components. My involvement was with the planning, Git management, some assistance with views, research on a API display issue, and the tag line "Turn On, Tune In, and Chart Out".
